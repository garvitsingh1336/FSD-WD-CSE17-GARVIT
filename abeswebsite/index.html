ABES Engineering College (ABESEC) is a technical institution offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and computer applications, including B.Tech, M.Tech, and MCA. The college is recognized for its strong focus on robotics and innovation, with its student teams achieving international acclaim. Notably, the ABESEC robotics team has participated in prestigious global competitions, including the International Aerial Robotics Competition in the USA and the Korean International Aerial Robot Aircraft competition, where they secured 4th position among 48 teams worldwide.
